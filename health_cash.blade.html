Exclusions

There are services that are not covered by this Health Insurance Policy.

These non-covered services are referred to as exclusions. Please ensure that you read and understand this section as the HMO shall not cover nor pay for expenses arising from these conditions

  • In-patient services (except lump sum payable for Antenatal/Delivery, Accident and Emergency).
  • Outpatient services not listed in Benefits table
  • Dental Care
  • Optical Care
  • Basic hematological investigations not related to covered services
  • Radiological investigations.

Exclusions

  • Advanced and Complex investigations
  • Surgeries not related to Maternity services or Accident and Emergency
  • Management of Cancers
  • Management of Pre-existing or Chronic illnesses
  • Professional Sports and willful exposure to needless danger
  • Cosmetic surgeries.
  • Conditions caused by an act of war or Beneficiary participation in a riot
  • Treatment for weight reduction or obesity.
  • All procedures, management and investigations not covered by the plan

Free Trial & Duration Period

A waiting and free trial period of 1 month applies.

We will complete your documentation and send your details to the health care provider.

During this time, you will be eligible for ONLY telemedicine services.

The policy shall run for one-year (the term) subject to uninterrupted monthly or annual premium payment and subsequently renewable.

Hospital Network

The HMO shall provide or through in-network hospitals arrange for provision to Beneficiaries of those Covered Services that are identified.

The Beneficiaries shall be entitled to healthcare at one (1) In-Network Hospital and can request for change of hospital bi-annually.

Compliance & Standards

All Covered Services shall be provided in accordance with the generally accepted clinical and legal standards consistent with best practices in medical ethics and practices applicable internationally based on the cover limit.

This service is provided by Casava Micro insurance Limited, a licensed and regulated General & Life micro insurance company. The policy is underwritten by Axa Health Insurance, an authorized HMO provider.
