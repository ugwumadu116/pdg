Making Claims

You must have an active Casava policy before you are eligible to make a claim. You must notify us of any event giving rise to a claim not more than 30 days after the disengagement.
You will provide evidence of entitlement to the benefit as we may require to properly process the claim. Such information may include, but is not limited to integration with bank accounts, disengagement letter, payslip and or bank statement.
If a claim is submitted by a policy holder within the first 4 months , such claim shall be disregarded as the wait period is not elapsed.

Cancel Policy

This policy can be terminated by you at anytime from the web platform.
We may terminate this policy if you violate our terms and conditions.
This policy is temporarily suspended if you do not pay your premium within 14 days from the expiration of your previous premium.
Your policy will be reactivated after you renew your premium. However, you may have a new waiting period.
